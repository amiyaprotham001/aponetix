FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We deliver web experience engineering, product development, SaaS solutions, and digital transformation advisory. Every project is custom-built around your specific goals.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple websites: 4-6 weeks. Product development: 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed estimates during discovery phase.

What is your development process?

We follow: Discover → Design → Develop → Optimize. Clear communication and iterative refinement ensures alignment with your vision throughout.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. We provide maintenance, optimization, and ongoing support packages. Discuss options during project planning.

What technologies do you use?

Modern, scalable tech stack: Python/Flask, React, Vue.js, Node.js, SQLite, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms like AWS/Cloudflare. Technology choices are always aligned with your project requirements and long-term maintenance goals.

How do you ensure project security?

Security-first approach: CSRF protection, rate limiting, secure authentication, SSL/TLS, regular audits, GDPR compliance, and secure deployment practices.
